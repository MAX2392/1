涉外案事件处置用语规范

为规范涉外案事件处置流程，保障执法工作顺利开展，特整理以下常用英汉双语用语规范，供铁路公安干警在工作中参考使用。

一、表明身份

汉：你好，我是本次列车乘警，这是我的证件。

英：Hello, I'm the police officer on this train. Here is my ID.

二、核实当事人信息

汉：请出示你的证件和车票

英：Please show me your passport and ticket.

汉：你的名字，国籍，生日，职业？

英：What's your name, nationality, date of birth, occupation?

汉：你是否享有外交特权和豁免权？

英：Do you enjoy diplomatic privileges and immunities？

汉：你的护照号码和有效日期？

英：The number of your passport and date of validity?

汉：你何时何地进入中国？

英：When and where did you enter China?

汉：你来中国的目的？

英：Your purpose of coming to China?

汉：自从来到中国，你一直在做什么？

英：What have you been doing since you arrived in China?

汉：请把以上信息写下来。

英：Please write down the above information.

三、面对记者

汉：不好意思，未经上级机关批准，我们不允许接受媒体记者采访，如有需要，请联系我们上级宣传部门。

英：Sorry, without the approval of the superior authority, we are not allowed to accept interviews from media reporters. If necessary, please contact our superior publicity department.

四、二次安检

汉：列车上正在开展二次安检，为了确保你能顺利出站，如果你随身携带有小刀，剪刀，充电宝，自喷压力容器等危险物品，请交给列车工作人员确认，谢谢配合。

英：A secondary security check is currently underway on the train. To ensure your smooth exit from the station, if you have any dangerous items such as knives, scissors, power banks, or self-discharging pressure vessels with you, please hand them over to the train staff for confirmation. Thank you for your cooperation.

汉：小刀和剪刀不能随身携带，列车工作人员将暂为保管。

英：Knives and scissors are not allowed to be carried on board. Train staff will keep them temporarily.

汉：这个充电宝能量超过100瓦时不能随身携带，列车工作人员将暂为保管。

英：This power bank with a capacity exceeding 100 watt-hours cannot be carried with you. Train staff will keep it temporarily.

汉：这罐发胶容积超过150ml不能随身携带，列车工作人员将暂为保管。

英：This can of hair spray has a capacity exceeding 150ml and cannot be carried on board. Train staff will keep it temporarily.
